About the Marathon

Running a marathon is not an event, it is a way of life.

The CT Half Marathon, a Race for Charity, was conceived with the vision & foresight of S. Charanjit Singh Channi ( Chairman, CT Group of Institutions ) to promote healthiness, robustness & fitness awareness. This marathon came into existence in 2007. Alongwith this race for a humanitarian cause, CT Group of Institutions raises funds for the organizations that work for the deprived & under privileged members of the society such as Pingalwara, Unique Home and other social upliftment Non Government Organizations. This laudable noble endeavour also encourages the physically challenged students or children like PRAYAS ( A School for Special students ) to participate in the race. CT Group of Institutions donates more than Rs. 5 lakh to the needy and deprived sections of society every year. Besides, the top 3 winners of CT Half Marathon are also conferred with Rs 21,000/- , Rs 11,000/- and Rs 5,100/- for both male and female positions, respectively.
